Sectors we serve
Specialist freight for specialist industries.

Entertainment and events are our core — but the same standard of care applies to every sector we work in.

Theatre & Touring

Sets, costumes, sound equipment, and lighting rigs between venues on tight turnarounds. We know how touring schedules work and what happens when a delivery is late.

Exhibitions & Conferences

Stands, displays, graphics, and samples delivered within your build-up window. We work to exhibition deadlines, not standard carrier timetables.

AV & Technical Equipment

Lighting rigs, LED walls, sound systems, and staging. Heavy, expensive, and fragile. Handled with the care that equipment of this value demands.

Set Builders & Scenic

Oversized loads, fragile painted finishes, workshop to venue and back. For set builders who cannot afford damage and cannot miss a build day.

Prop Houses

Regular collections between your store and production sets. Reliable, consistent, and familiar with the pace and requirements of film and TV schedules.

General Freight

Commercial, industrial, and ad-hoc loads across the UK and Europe. If it needs moving and needs to be managed properly, we can help.

Process
From enquiry to proof of delivery.

Every job follows the same process. No surprises, no chasing for updates. You will know what is happening at each stage without having to ask.

Request a quote
01
Enquiry

Tell us what you need to move, where it is going, and when. We will come back with a quote — usually within a few hours, often less.

02
Booking confirmed

We confirm the carrier, vehicle type, and collection timings. You receive a job reference and direct contact details.

03
Collection

Driver arrives at the agreed time. Consignment note is signed. You are notified when collection is confirmed.

In transit

We monitor the shipment and keep you informed. If anything changes, you hear about it before it becomes a problem.

POD & invoice

Signed proof of delivery sent to you on completion. Invoice issued the same day. Clean, simple, done.

About Novaline
A straightforward operation, built around the work.

Novaline is a UK-based freight forwarding and transport coordination business, currently operating as a sole trader and built to grow into a full limited company. We work with businesses that need freight handled reliably, communicated clearly, and coordinated by someone who takes ownership of the job.

Our focus is entertainment and events logistics — a sector where the margins for error are small and the cargo is often irreplaceable. That discipline runs through everything we do.

What this means in practice
Direct coordination on every job — no intermediaries between you and the person managing your freight.
Bilingual capability in English and French, supporting UK–Europe movements without communication barriers.
A growing network of vetted carriers and industry contacts across the UK and Europe.
Experience in sectors where timing, handling, and communication are non-negotiable.
No minimum spend, no contracts. We earn your repeat business through the quality of the work.
